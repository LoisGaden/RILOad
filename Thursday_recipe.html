Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Kidney beans
  • Tin of chopped tomatoes
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Oregano
  • Frozen peas
  • Dark chocolate

Method

Dice the onion and garlic and chop your tomatoes into chunks.

Add a splash of oil to the pan and fry the onion then garlic. Let cook for 5 mins.

Add the kidney beans and chuck in a generous amount of cumin, paprika and chilli (optional). Mix it up to make sure it's coated with spices. Add half a can of chopped tomatoes (you can use the other half in our chickpea baked eggs or sausage meatballs). Cover pan and leave on low heat to cook for 10-15 mins.

While it cooks, boil the kettle and put your rice on. Cook according to packet. After 10 mins you can throw in some frozen peas if you have any to add extra veg.

Add a square of dark chocolate to the chilli. Make sure it's mixed in well. Remove your chilli from the heat and drain the rice - eat and enjoy!
