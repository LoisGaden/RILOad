Easy spaghetti meatballs

  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tin chopped tomatoes
  • Linda McCartney sausages
  • Oregano
  • Pasta of your choice

Preheat the oven to 180 C ready for the Lindas.

Dice the onion and garlic. Add a glug of oil to a saucepan on medium heat and wait a couple of minutes for it to get hot. Add the onion and cook for around 5 minutes.

TOP TIP: if the onion looks like it's burning add a splash of water to the pan, this stops them burning and helps them to soften. Add the garlic and cook for another 2 minutes.

Add half a tin of chopped tomatoes (you can save the other half for chickpea baked eggs or chilli sin carne)recipes. Reduce the heat and add a spoon of mixed herbs for flavour. Cover the pan and leave to simmer.

While your sauce cooks, stick two Linda McCartney sausages into the oven. As your Lindas and sauce cook, bring a pan of water to the boil (use the kettle to speed this up) and add your pasta. Cook according to instructions.

Drain the pasta when it's done. Take your Lindas out of the oven and cut them into chunks. Add the sausage chunks to your tomato sauce and salt and pepper to taste.
